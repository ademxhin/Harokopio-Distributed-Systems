Carpooling between City and University

GreenRide is a carpooling web application that helps students and employees share rides to and from the university or the city center. Each user can act as a driver or as a passenger, using a single account.

As a driver, you can create rides by setting departure point, destination, time and available seats. As a passenger, you can search for available rides, book a seat and cancel your booking when needed. The system keeps a basic history of past rides and allows simple ratings between drivers and passengers.

An administrator can monitor the platform, handle malicious accounts and view simple statistics such as number of rides and average occupancy. The goal is to make everyday commuting more efficient, cheaper and more eco-friendly.
